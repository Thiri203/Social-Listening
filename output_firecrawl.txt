## The Viriyah Insurance join the Royal Thai Army on National Children's Day 2023

     General Narongphan Jitkaewthae, Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Thai Army (Cmdr.) presided over the ceremony. Honored to visit the company's activity booth The Viriyah Insurance Public Company Limited, with Ms. Kanda Wattanayingsomsuk Marketing consultant for car manufacturers and dealers welcome Inside the National Children's Day event of the year 2023 Organized by the Royal Thai Army, the company also brought a group of volunteer spirits to create happiness and fun through game activities that inserted knowledge about road safety. Including preparing snacks to replenish energy for the children, including ice cream and grilled meatballs. In addition, the company also sponsored 20 bicycles as a gift for Children's Day at the area within the 2nd Cavalry Brigade, King's Guard, Phaholyothin Road, Samsen Subdistrict, Phayathai District, Bangkok.
